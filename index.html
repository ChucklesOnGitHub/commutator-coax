Open Ephys Commutators#

This is the documentation site for the Open Ephys Commutators, our range of open-source active near-zero torque commutators to prevent tether twisting during freely moving recordings of animals for coaxial and SPI interface cables.

It is currently under construction as of Jan 2022

FeaturesÂ¶

  • Near zero-torque commutation (no pull on the tether needed!)

  • Automatic compensation using head-mounted orientation sensors or orientation tracking

  • Remote control via open-source software as well as manual control buttons

  • Silent operation thanks to the voltage-controlled advanced stepper driver

  • Precise motion using step interpolation (256 uSteps/step)

  • Optical table and 80/20 rail mountable

  • Single USB connection for power and control

Where to buy one#

You can order Commutators from Open Ephys Production Site , as well as compatible acquisition systems, headstages and miniscopes.
